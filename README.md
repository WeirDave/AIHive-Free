# AI Hive — Free Edition

**AI Hive Free** is the open, manual version of AI Hive — a multi-AI collaboration workspace that lets you orchestrate multiple AI assistants on any problem or document.

No API keys. No install. No accounts needed on the AI Hive side. Just open it and go.

➡️ **[Launch AI Hive Free](https://weirdave.github.io/AIHive-Free/)**

---

## What It Does

Throw any question, problem, or document at multiple AIs simultaneously. Collect their responses. Let a Builder AI consolidate everything. Iterate as many rounds as you need.

This is how AI Hive started — the original manual workflow that inspired the Pro version.

Works for:
- Brainstorming ideas
- Getting multiple opinions on a problem
- Collaborative document drafting and refinement
- Any open-ended question you want six different takes on

---

## How To Use It

**Step 1** — Enter your project name and goal (or question)

**Step 2** — Check which AIs you want to use. Toggle any on or off. Add custom AIs with the ＋ button.

**Step 3** — Click **⚡ Build & Copy Prompt** — AI Hive assembles the full prompt for you

**Step 4** — Open each AI in its own browser tab, paste the prompt, copy the response back into its card

**Step 5** — Once responses are in, click **⚡ Build & Copy Prompt** again — AI Hive now builds the Builder prompt, combining all responses

**Step 6** — Paste that into your chosen Builder AI, copy the result back into the Working Document

**Step 7** — Click **Next Round →** and repeat

## Free AIs That Work

Any AI with a free web interface works:
- [ChatGPT](https://chatgpt.com)
- [Claude](https://claude.ai)
- [DeepSeek](https://chat.deepseek.com)
- [Gemini](https://gemini.google.com)
- [Grok](https://grok.com)
- [Perplexity](https://www.perplexity.ai)

You can also add any custom AI with the ＋ Add button.
